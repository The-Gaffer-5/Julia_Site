Telehealth

Telehealth uses videoconferencing in order to provide health care at a distance. It helps eliminate distance barriers, can improve access to medical services, and enhance care.

- With telehealth every appointment is confidential and meets HIPPA requirements.
- Appointments are made online and are at a time convenient for you while at your home or office.
- Telehealth provides easy access to your psychiatric provider.

Telehealth is available to patients who are:

- Age 19 or older
- Vermont resident
- Requesting help with depression or anxiety
- Not currently taking or requesting controlled substances, such as benzodiazepines or psychostimulants

Is telehealth confidential?

Doxy.me meets all HIPPA requirements, the federal requirements for patient confidentiality and privacy. At the time of your appointment, select a private location and a secure internet connection. Privacy cannot be guaranteed if you choose to meet with your provider at a public location.

I will be traveling out of state. Can I schedule an appointment for telehealth while I am away?

Unfortunately, due to telehealth regulatory requirements, you must be physically present in Vermont at the time of your appointment.

Can I schedule a telehealth appointment for ADHD?

At this time, only patients who are requesting help with anxiety or depression can be scheduled for telehealth. Evaluation for ADHD medications, such as Adderall or Ritalin, requires a traditional in-person appointment.
