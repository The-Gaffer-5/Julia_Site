New Patients

New Patient Telephone Consultation

10 minutes
Schedule a phone call to speak with Julia about your request for treatment. This is a brief opportunity to see if Julia will be a good fit for you and your needs.
$0

Psychiatric Diagnostic Interview

60 minutes
This appointment is for new patients wishing to establish care. A comprehensive review of your present concerns and pertinent history will be reviewed. The need for ongoing treatment will be determined and, if appropriate, psychiatric medication will be prescribed.
$225

Psychiatric Consultation

80 minutes
This appointment is for patients who would like a psychiatric consultation but intend to have their primary care provider prescribe their psychiatric medication. During the appointment, your presenting difficulties will be assessed and a comprehensive treatment plan, which can be followed by your primary care provider, will be developed. Prior consent of your primary care provider is necessary. Please contact Julia (insert link) for more information.
$300

Psychiatric Deprescribing

75 minutes
This appointment is appropriate for patients wishing to decrease their psychiatric medication. Current response to medication, potential risk factors, and current psychiatric stability will be evaluated. If appropriate, a psychiatric withdrawal plan will be developed. Please note that psychiatric withdrawal requires close monitoring and patients will be expected to schedule monthly pharmacologic management appointments while they are undergoing a taper of their medications.
$250

Follow-Up Appointments

Pharmacologic Management

25 minutes
This appointment is for established patients and is an opportunity to review your response to psychiatric medication and to make any necessary adjustments. Brief counselling may also occur.
$100

Medication Management/Psychotherapy

50 minutes
For patient requesting more comprehensive treatment, pharmacologic management can be paired with psychotherapy.
$200

Telemental Health

Telemental health is an opportunity to receive psychiatric care via video appointment and is ideal for patients with challenging work schedules or for those who live in remote areas. Telemental health is available to Vermont residents only. It is not available to Medicare patients.
*Psychotherapy is not currently available through telemental health. Learn more
