About Julia

Julia is an adult psychiatric nurse practitioner with over 15 years of experience in outpatient mental health. She sees adult patients, ages 19 or older. Patients best suited to her practice are adults requesting assistance with depression, anxiety, psychological trauma, bipolar disorder or other mood symptoms. Julia also works with patients, who are and have been stable, to reduce their psychiatric medications.

Julia does not provide outpatient detoxification, forensic or emergency services. Individuals in need of frequent crisis management may benefit from agencies with more extensive or integrated therapeutic resources.
